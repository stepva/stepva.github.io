It took me 23 years to start drinking coffee. In March 2022 I decided I needed a new hobby. Coffee was (and still is) something misterious to me which I'm now attempting to discover, understand and enjoy. I decided to set up this as a coffee diary, keeping track of the coffee I had, its origins and story, what I liked and disliked and what I learned.
